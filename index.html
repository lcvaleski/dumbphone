FAQ

How does it work?

We install supervised profiles on your iPhone using Apple's Mobile Device Management (MDM) system. This is the same technology used by schools and enterprises to manage devices.

The supervised profile permanently restricts app installation and website access based on your selections. Once configured, these restrictions cannot be removed without our supervision certificate.

Concerned about monitoring or privacy?

We cannot and do not monitor your personal data. Apple's MDM architecture specifically prevents supervision profiles from accessing:

  • Messages, calls, or personal communications
  • Photos, files, or personal content
  • Location data or browsing history
  • App-specific data or passwords

Why can't I just do this myself?

Yes. Switching eSIMs between phones takes less than 5 minutes. Our phones make excellent secondary devices for:

  • Digital detox weekends
  • Evening wind-down device
