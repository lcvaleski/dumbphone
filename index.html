We install a special configuration on your child's iPhone that permanently controls which apps and websites they can access. This uses the same secure technology that schools and companies use for their devices.

Once we set up these restrictions based on your choices, your child cannot bypass, disable, or remove them. Only you can request changes through your Parent Account.

We cannot and do not monitor your personal data. Apple's security architecture specifically prevents our configuration from accessing:

  • Messages, calls, or personal communications
  • Photos, files, or personal content
  • Location data or browsing history
  • App-specific data or passwords
